OUR STORY

Admission Network Ltd functions under our parent company First Kare Academy Ltd and First Kare Ltd. First Kare Academy and First Kare Ltd were both incorporated in the year 2014 where we are a ROTO, RoATP, and Matrix accredited training center. We are offering Government-funded and Private-funded courses ranging from Care Certificate courses, Level-2 and Level-3 Business, Health and Social Care, Child Care, and Hospitality Management. First Kare Academy also offers Functional Skills Maths and English Entry level 1 to Entry level 3 and Level 1 & 2.

First Kare Ltd is a recruitment company focusing on the Health and Social Care sector. Since 2014, over 5000 learners have successfully completed courses, with 70% gaining admission to UK Colleges and Universities. The academy recruits over 100 learners monthly and maintains strong connections with the local community, employers, and recruiters. The long-term goal involves expanding Admission Network to represent over 100 countries, facilitating easy access to admission processes and academic progression, aligning with a 5-year plan for global development.

HOW WE WORK

Our students have the opportunity to choose and apply to a higher education course from a range of universities and colleges that match their educational background, experience & interests. Our partner schools, colleges, and universities will gain a diversified campus by bringing in quality students from around the world.

Our recruitment partners are encouraged to give your students a chance to successfully access and pursue their education in a range of prestigious educational institutions around the world.

Our global team of experts provides students with support at every step, including guidance for their applications, ensuring all necessary documents are submitted, communicating with the universities and colleges, assisting with the visa process for a successful application process to begin their journey in higher education.
